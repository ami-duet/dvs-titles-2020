A job title is a term that describes the position held by us as an employee, or a freelancer. Mostly it reflects the level of the position, our progression - junior to expert - and the responsibilities we handle. Your title can be just a few words, less or much more. In our 2020’s survey, DVS members have listed 911 different titles, as well as variations, starting from 2- letter titles to 82-letter!

How would you answer to “Tell me about your job title?” — In this special edition, we wish to visually decode relations between your job title and other parameters.
